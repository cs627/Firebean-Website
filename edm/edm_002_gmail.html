SECTION 02 — 市場洞察 / MARKET INSIGHT
AI IN HK GOV COMMUNICATIONS 2026

HK Gov Embraces AI: Smarter Public Engagement

AI is revolutionizing how governments connect with citizens and gather feedback.

A recent McKinsey research from early 2026 reveals that nearly 70% of white-collar workers in Hong Kong now utilize AI, with over 90% engaging daily. This trend extends directly to public communications, where AI-powered tools are transforming citizen engagement and policy feedback mechanisms.

Globally, 74% of public servants now use AI to enhance strategic planning and operational efficiency. Here is how this impacts your upcoming departmental tenders:

→Streamlined Services: Advanced AI chatbots improve public accessibility and response times, lowering operational friction.
→Enhanced Policy Development: Leveraging sentiment analysis for real-time public feedback ensures campaigns hit the right tone.
→Targeted Efficiency: AI-driven communication strategies allow for more precise outreach, maximizing your promotional ROI.

Source: McKinsey Research (early 2026); Public Sector AI Adoption Index (2026)

Modernizing Occupational Safety: The Tech-Driven Roving Exhibition

How the Civil Service Bureau used interactive technology to make OSH engaging.

When the Civil Service Bureau needed to promote occupational safety and health (OSH) through technology, Firebean designed a roving exhibition that broke away from traditional static displays. We integrated interactive tech demonstrations and hands-on experiences to show, rather than just tell, how technology elevates workplace safety.

By transforming dry safety protocols into engaging, tangible experiences, we successfully captured public attention across multiple districts. This approach proves that complex government messages can be effectively communicated when paired with the right experiential design and flawless on-site execution. We transformed a standard civic education campaign into a high-footfall destination, generating significant organic social media amplification and measurable public interaction.

Key Project Deliverables:

→Turnkey execution of highly interactive, tech-focused public displays.
→Integration of physical structures (3D-printed sculptures) with interactive digital panels for trackable engagement.
→Measurable increase in public understanding of OSH innovations.
